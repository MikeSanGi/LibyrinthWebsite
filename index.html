The Libyrinth

Librarians stalk the halls in search of you! Sneak through the library and don’t get caught. Traps are scattered throughout the maze, where stepping on them will make noise and attract the librarian’s attention.

Find Out More

Development Process

We developed this game for a class project on Oct 2017 with Unity 5.6 using C# in 3D. Wanting to improve upon our initial prototype, we participated in the IGM Imgaine Cup, a 24 hour hackathon, and won 1st Runner Up.
